Common documents required for student visa applications include a valid passport, university acceptance letter, proof of financial capability (bank statements, scholarship letters, sponsor documents), academic transcripts, standardized test scores, visa application forms, passport-sized photographs, and proof of health insurance. Our visa specialists will provide you with a country-specific checklist to ensure you have all necessary documents.

Ideally, you should start the application process 12-18 months before your intended start date. This timeline allows for:

  • 12-18 months before: Research universities and programs, prepare for and take standardized tests (IELTS/TOEFL, GRE/GMAT).
  • 10-12 months before: Finalize university shortlist, begin working on personal statements and essays.
  • 8-10 months before: Request recommendation letters, prepare application materials.
  • 6-8 months before: Submit applications (for fall intake).
  • 4-6 months before: Receive admission decisions, apply for scholarships.
  • 3-4 months before: Apply for student visa, arrange accommodation.
  • 1-2 months before: Make travel arrangements, attend pre-departure orientation.

Starting early gives you adequate time for test preparation, thoughtful applications, and addressing any unexpected challenges that may arise.

Application deadlines vary by country, university, and program, but here are some general guidelines:

  • USA: Early Decision/Action: November-December; Regular Decision: January-February for fall intake (August/September start).
  • UK: UCAS deadlines are typically October 15 for Oxford/Cambridge and medical programs, January 15 for most undergraduate programs. Postgraduate deadlines vary by university.
  • Canada: January-February for fall intake, though some universities have rolling admissions.
  • Australia: October-November for February intake; April-May for July intake.
  • Europe: Varies widely, but many programs have deadlines between January and April for fall intake.

Many universities also offer spring/winter intakes with deadlines typically 6 months before the start date. Our counsellors will provide you with specific deadlines for your target universities and programs.
